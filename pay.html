Ways To Pay



Pay for Phoenix flights easily and safely with your credit or debit card, Apple Pay, UAE NetBanking, UATP, or through cash



Credit or debit card


Once you've booked your flight online or through our Customer Centre, you can pay quickly and securely using MasterCard, Visa or American Express.
When you pay using a card, you’ll be charged an administration fee for the booking. Phoenix will also ask to see the card at the airport before you board your flight.


Please note:

  • If a credit/debit card has been used to pay on behalf of someone else, we'll need to see a photocopy of the front and back of the credit or debit card and a photocopy of the cardholder's passport at the airport.
  • If the passenger is unable to produce the photocopies at the airport, flydubai will not allow the passenger to travel.

Credit or debit cards can’t be used to pay on behalf of someone else travelling to or from Bangladesh, Ethiopia, Nepal, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Sudan or South Sudan.



Cash


If you wish to pay using cash, select the ‘Pay later’ option when you book your flight online or through our Customer Centre. You can pay cash at a Phoenix Travel shop or through one of our payment partners.


Please note:

  • A cash payment fee may apply.
  • If your flight departure is within 48 hours from the time of booking, you must make the payment immediately.
  • If you’re travelling in Economy Class, your booking will be on hold for 24 hours allowing you time to make the payment. You must pay for your flight within 24 hours.
  • If you’re travelling in Business Class, you’ll have 24 hours to make the payment.
  • If your flight booking has both Economy and Business Class as part of your trip, you must pay for your flight within 24 hours.



Apple Pay


When booking through the Phoenix Mobile App you can choose to pay using Apple Pay. Making a payment with Apple Pay is simple and works with most Apple devices. Simply add your debit or credit card and pay for your flight safely and securely.




UAE Netbanking


UAE NetBanking is an online payment system introduced by the Central Bank of the United Arab Emirates. It allows you to pay for your flight directly with your bank account in the UAE.




UATP

Universal Air Travel Plan (UATP) is an airline-owned payment network for business organisations. It is an alternative form of payment accepted by over 250 airlines and thousands of travel agencies globally. UATP allows corporations to manage their travel expenses and streamline billing by providing detailed reports of air travel.




Pay on someone else's behalf


With Phoenix you can pay for another traveller’s flight. Simply select the ‘Pay later’ option when you book a flight, and make a cash payment through one of our Payment partners.
If you wish to pay using a debit or credit card, the passenger you are paying for will need to show a photocopy of the front and the back of your card, and a photocopy of your passport at check-in.
You can’t use a credit or debit card to pay on behalf of someone else travelling to or from Bangladesh, Ethiopia, Nepal, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, Sudan or South Sudan.




Interest-free instalment plans


We've partnered with Emirates NBD and RAK Bank to offer you 0% instalment plans.



Emirates NBD 0% instalment plans


With Emirates NBD’s 0% instalment plan, you can pay for your booking of AED 1,000 or above in 3-month or 6-month instalments when you make a booking via flydubai.com, the Phoenix Customer Centre or through one of Phoenix’s travel shops. To find out more or to convert your purchase into a 0% instalment plan contact Emirates NBD on 800-ENBD with details of your transaction.



RAK Bank 0% Easy Payment Plan


With RAK Bank’s 0% Easy Payment Plan, you’ll be able to pay for your booking in 3-month or 6-month instalments. Simply make a booking of AED 1,000 or above via Phoenix.com, the Phoenix Customer Centre or through one of Phoenix’s travel shops. To find out more or to convert your purchase into a 0% instalment plan, contact RAK Bank on 23456700000.
