Child and infact fares



  • Child fares follow the same fee structure as adult fares and depend on the booking channel.
  • When booked on the Phoenix website, via flydubai Customer Centre or a Phoenix travel partner, the infant fare will be AED 75 per sector plus taxes.
  • For connecting flights (bookings with multiple sectors for a single flight), the infant fare will be AED 150 per sector plus taxes.



Rebooking and cancellation rules for Phoenix fares



  • All rebooking and cancellation fees are per person, per sector.
  • In case of full or partial rebooking or cancellation, any difference in fare plus rebooking or cancellation fee needs to be paid.
  • Partial cancellation is not allowed when the journey hasn't started. If you haven't started your journey and still want to cancel any of the flight sectors, we'll need to cancel the entire booking.
  • Connection bookings can't be converted to bookings with 'stopover'.
  • Cancellation refunds will be issued in the form of Phoenix voucher which can be used to pay for flydubai flights or optional extras before the expiry date mentioned on the voucher.
  • For non-refundable fares, only applicable taxes will be refunded.
  • Cancellation of optional extras (meal, baggage, seat) is allowed only 24 hours prior to departure.
