Economy Class

Home Fly With Us Economy Class

You can expect an affordable and comfortable journey when you fly with us in Phoenix’s Economy Class. The cabin is spacious and bright providing you a relaxing atmosphere throughout your flight.

Relax in comfortable seats


Our economy class seats are high quality and their ergonomic comfort and innovative design sets them apart. With the seat pocket at the top of the seat, you’ll get more legroom to stretch out and relax. For a small additional cost, you can even choose a seat with extra legroom.

Be entertained


Choose from the very best Hollywood, Bollywood and international movies, TV shows, music, games and news. With over 2,000 hours of entertainment available, there’s something to keep everyone entertained.

Grab a bite to eat


A variety of snacks and beverages is available for purchase from our onboard menu on all our flights.

Stay connected


We’re rolling out WiFi across our fleet, so you can now stay in touch with your friends, catch up on your emails or get updates on your favourite sport matches. You can buy a WiFi package on board and access the internet using your phone, tablet or laptop.
